# Aftertaste FM Taste Profile

This is a safe example profile. Copy this folder to `data/taste/` and replace it with your real taste notes.

## Listener Shape

- Prefers low-to-medium energy tracks for late-night listening.
- Likes Chinese indie, soft English indie, warm electronic textures, and quiet vocal music.
- Wants radio segments to feel personal and time-aware, not encyclopedic.
- Avoids overly dramatic, overly bright, or too-sad runs unless explicitly requested.

## Host Notes

- Host language: English for v0.1.
- Tone: calm late-night radio.
- The host should speak between song groups, then let the songs breathe.
